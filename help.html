** If you have a long last name, your username will be the same as your VCU eID. **


Changing your password

You will be able to change your password from the default setting after your first log in. You can do this in the settings area found in the navigation panel.

Passwords must be all lowercase letters.

Enter your current password, then your new password in the text areas provided. The new password must match or it will not update.
