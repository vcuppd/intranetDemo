Basic Reminders About Office Protocol at the Partnership

Revised April 2009
  • Office hours are 8 am to 5pm. Doors will be locked before and after those hours.
  • Please call supervisors ( and leave voicemail if necessary) with any change in plans (unexpectedly absent, late, etc.).
  • Annual leave must be submitted and approved in advance. Sick leave must be submitted as soon as possible after the fact
  • Submit hourly time sheets on timeâ€”a day before if possible.
  • Use the in/out board daily and keep your itinerary up-to-date.
  • Submit website updates (training dates/descriptions; project descriptions) to Charles and Joanne in a timely manner.
  • For core annual report/NIRS: Check all program descriptions and update them, check to be sure activities are entered. Data need to be entered for June 30.
